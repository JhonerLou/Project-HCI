Gameloft ID Update
and Maintenance

26 May 2024
8 Minutes

We’re happy to announce we’re taking the next steps in our Gameloft ID release. On May 27th, we’ll be releasing Gameloft ID on Android and iOS devices. This means that our Android and iOS players will finally be able to create their own Gameloft IDs, allowing them to save their account progress and switch between different devices. For now, cross-platform will still be limited, and you will only be able to switch from android device to android device, or iOS device to iOS device. To enable full cross-platform we will need to migrate all accounts to the new Asphalt Legends Unite game-space, and we will be starting with our PC accounts next Wednesday.
